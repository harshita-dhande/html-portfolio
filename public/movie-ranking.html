The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Saw series

The Saw series is a horror franchise that revolves around the character John Kramer, also known as the Jigsaw Killer. The series is known for its intricate traps and moral dilemmas, where victims must make life-or-death choices to escape.

Zindagi na Milegi Dobara

Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara is a Hindi film that follows three friends on a road trip in Spain. The film explores themes of friendship, self-discovery, and living life to the fullest.

Hereditary

Hereditary is a psychological horror film that delves into the dark secrets of a family after the death of their secretive grandmother. The film is known for its unsettling atmosphere and exploration of grief and trauma.
